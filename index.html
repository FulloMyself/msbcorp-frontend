About MSB Finance

MSB Finance was founded by our CEO, Mr. Lebogang Mosebi, whose journey with money began at an early age. As a child, he discovered the value of saving when he realized that setting aside just R2 of his lunch money each day could grow into R40 by the end of the month. That simple lesson shaped his passion for financial literacy and inspired him to build a company dedicated to empowering communities through responsible lending.

At MSB Finance, we believe that “money is like a soldier—when deployed wisely, it protects, conquers, and builds.” Our mission is to ensure every loan we provide becomes a tool for growth, stability, and opportunity for our clients.

Frequently Asked Questions

What do I need to qualify for a loan?

  • Valid South African ID
  • Latest 3 months bank statements and payslip
  • Permanent employment with monthly salary
  • Valid cell phone number and bank account
  • Must be 18 years or older

How much can I borrow?

New clients can borrow between R300 and R2000. Repeat clients may borrow up to R4000. Loans are available 24 hours a day, 6 days a week (Saturdays off; applications made on Saturday are processed on Sunday).

How long before I receive the money?

Once your loan and DebiCheck are approved, funds are paid the same day and typically appear in your account within 24 hours.

What if I have a bad credit record?

A credit check is performed, but a negative record does not automatically disqualify you. Other factors are considered to determine your repayment ability.

Will I receive cash or bank deposit?

All loans are deposited directly into your active bank account. Cash payouts are not available.

Who is MSB Finance accredited with?

MSB Finance is fully accredited with the National Credit Regulator (NCR), allowing us to provide short-term loans across South Africa.

Can I qualify if I have existing debt?

Loan eligibility is evaluated for each application, even with existing debt. Accurate information is essential, and we encourage you to contact us with questions.

What if I cannot repay on time?

Life events happen. Communicate with us to explore alternative arrangements to manage your repayment schedule.

What happens if I don’t repay at all?

Failure to repay without communication will result in legal recovery processes, which may include debt collectors and additional costs. This may affect future loan eligibility.

For additional questions, contact us at info@msbfinance.co.za.
