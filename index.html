About MSB Finance

MSB Finance was founded by our CEO, Mr. Lebogang Mosebi, whose journey with money began at an early age. As a child, he discovered the value of saving when he realized that setting aside just R2 of his lunch money each day could grow into R40 by the end of the month. That simple lesson shaped his passion for financial literacy and inspired him to build a company dedicated to empowering communities through responsible lending.

At MSB Finance, we believe that “money is like a soldier—when deployed wisely, it protects, conquers, and builds.” Our mission is to ensure every loan we provide becomes a tool for growth, stability, and opportunity for our clients.
